    Avocado Toast

    Description: A simple and delicious breakfast recipe.

    Ingredients:

    • 2 slices of bread
    • 1 avocado
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:

    1. Toast the bread.
    2. Mash the avocado.
    3. Spread the avocado on the toast.

    Omelette

    Description: A classic breakfast recipe.

    Ingredients:

    • 2 eggs
    • 1 cup of shredded cheese
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:

    1. Beat the eggs.
    2. Add the cheese.
    3. Cook in a pan.
